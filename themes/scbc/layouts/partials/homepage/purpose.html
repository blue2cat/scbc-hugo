Our Purpose

We gather together to worship the Lord and strengthen one another with Bible-teaching and fellowship.
We depart to live for Christ and share His Gospel throughout the week.

Expository Preaching

We preach Christ through verse-by-verse exposition of the Bible (1 Cor 2:2).

Scripture

Our authority is the Bible which is God's inspired, inerrant, infallible Word (2 Tim 3:16).

Love for One Another

We care for one another with the love of Christ (John 13:34).

Worship

We gather together to glorify the eternal God through worship (Isaiah 6:3).

Grow

Through the ministry of the Word of God and fellowship, we seek to honor Christ with our lives (2 Cor 3:18).

Reach Out

We reach out to the community and the world bringing the Gospel of Christ to the dying and lost (Matt 28:19-20).
